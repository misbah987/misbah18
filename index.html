Cats

Cats are very cute animals.Cats have been domesticated for around 4,000 years.While they were once valued for their hunting abilities, they are now valued for their companionship and loving behaviour.Teeth of cats are sharper when they're kittens. After six months, they lose their needle-sharp milk teeth.A group of kittens is called a "kindle", and "clowder" is a term that refers to a group of adult cats.A female cat is also known to be called a "queen" or a "molly."Around the world, cats take a break to nap a catnap 425 million times a day.
